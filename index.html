The approach

Not performance.
Not manipulation.
Just truth, told
with care.

I make films that help people feel seen without making them feel watched. The aim is to create space for honesty, then shape that honesty into something cinematic and memorable.

The camera should not dominate the room. The story should.

About

Inside the room.

I'm a full-time filmmaker and director based in Glasgow, working across documentary, sport and cultural film.

I shoot, direct and edit lean productions that still feel considered, cinematic and emotionally grounded.

The work is built around trust: getting close enough to the subject that the film feels lived-in rather than staged.
